Synchronized swimming is a very difficult sport. It is mix of swimming and acrobatics. Imagine yourself performing graceful, acrobatic movements in the water in tact with music while holding your breath and constantly moving around the pool and even at the bottom of it! If you want to be competitor, you will need extremely strong muscles and the ability to hold your breath for long periods of time. But if you are interested and willing to try, then do not hesitate and dare!
